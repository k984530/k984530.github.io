Which Should I Pick?

Use this quick guide before you open the store checkout.

Choose a credit pack

Pick a one-time pack when you want to test Kova, make a few profile options, or create occasional social posts without a monthly commitment.

Choose a monthly plan

Pick a membership when Kova is part of a recurring creator workflow, profile refresh workflow, or content batch process where you expect to generate every month.

Questions

Short answers for users deciding whether to try Kova.

Do I need to write prompts?

No. Kova is designed around ready-made style choices, so you can start from a photo instead of writing a detailed prompt.

Can I use Kova without subscribing?

Credit packs are available for flexible use. Monthly plans are available for people who want a recurring creation workflow.

Where do I see the exact price?

The final local price is shown in the App Store or Google Play checkout before you confirm any purchase.
