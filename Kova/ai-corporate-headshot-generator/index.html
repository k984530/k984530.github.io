Is this for individual users or companies?

Both. Individuals can install Kova and try a professional headshot style. Companies should use Team Headshot Sprint when they need consistent employee headshots and commercial usage clarity.

Can the portraits be used on company pages?

Yes, after the agreed commercial usage scope is confirmed before invoice. Typical uses include LinkedIn, company directories, team pages, hiring pages, and launch decks.

Does this send anything automatically?

No. The invoice path only prepares an email draft and does not submit a form or send a buyer message by itself. The AI Creator Prompt Pack button opens Gumroad for buyers who want a lower-cost self-serve kit.
