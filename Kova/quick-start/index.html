Pick A Better First Photo

The first photo matters more than the first style. Use this quick check before you spend credits.

Clear subject

A single face, pet, outfit, or product usually gives the cleanest first result.

Enough light

Start with a photo that is not too dark, blurry, or heavily cropped.
