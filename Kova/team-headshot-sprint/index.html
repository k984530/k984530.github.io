Questions

Fast answers for teams comparing a self-serve AI photo app with a paid profile-photo service.

Is this different from the Kova app?

Yes. The app is self-serve. Team Headshot Sprint is a paid service for teams that want consistent direction, selection help, and business-use clarity.

What photos do we need?

Each person should provide one clear phone photo, plus the startup team page or LinkedIn profile direction the portraits need to match.

Can we use the final images commercially?

Commercial usage scope is confirmed before invoice, including LinkedIn profiles, company team pages, hiring pages, or launch decks.

Start Smaller Or Go Higher Touch

Individuals can install Kova and test starter credits first. Teams can use the sprint when the profile-photo problem has business value and a deadline.
