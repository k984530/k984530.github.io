Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sweet Sleep handles information for the mobile app, website, and support requests. Contact: hello@choiwon.dev.

Information We Collect

  • Support information you send to us, such as your email address, device model, app version, issue details, screenshots, or crash descriptions.
  • Store information made available by Apple App Store or Google Play when needed for installation, purchase, or download-related support.
  • Technical website request metadata and ordinary diagnostics needed to operate the public Sweet Sleep pages.
  • Sleep preferences, routines, reminders, sounds, or sleep notes may be stored locally on your device depending on the app features you use.

How We Use Information

  • To provide sleep routine, relaxation, reminder, and app support features.
  • To respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • To maintain app store listings, public documentation, and service reliability.

Health and Sleep Data

Sweet Sleep is a sleep routine and relaxation tool, not a medical provider. Do not send sensitive medical records by email unless needed for support and you are comfortable sharing them. If you have a medical concern about insomnia or sleep quality, consult a qualified health professional.

Sharing

We do not sell user data. We may share limited information with service providers only as needed to operate the app, respond to support, comply with law, protect rights and safety, or handle store-related support through Apple or Google.

Deletion and Contact

You can request deletion of support emails or related support records by contacting hello@choiwon.dev. Some records may be retained where required for security, dispute resolution, legal compliance, or ordinary business records.
