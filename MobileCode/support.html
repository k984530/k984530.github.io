Connection Checklist

  1. Open the desktop server on your computer.
  2. Confirm that your phone and computer can reach each other over the selected network.
  3. Enter the connection code exactly as shown.
  4. If connection fails, restart the server and check firewall or VPN settings.

Safety

Remote access controls your computer. Use trusted networks, protect connection codes, and disconnect sessions when finished.
