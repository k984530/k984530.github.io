Privacy Policy

Star Diary

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Overview

Star Diary is a private diary app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles diary content, subscription processing, ads, and support requests.

Diary Content

Diary entries are stored on your device by default. Star Diary does not require an account and does not intentionally upload diary text to our servers in the current release.

Subscriptions and RevenueCat

Star Diary Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play billing and managed through RevenueCat. RevenueCat may process purchase identifiers, entitlement status, product identifiers, device information needed for purchase validation, and related subscription events.

Advertising and AdMob

Free users may see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may process advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, ad impressions, and ad interaction data according to Google's advertising policies and user consent settings.

Support Requests

If you contact support, we may receive your email address, app version, device details you choose to provide, and the content of your message. We use this information only to respond to your request and improve Star Diary.

Children

Star Diary is not directed to children under 13. If you are under the age of digital consent in your region, use the app only with a parent or guardian's permission.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management.
  • Google AdMob for advertising in the free version.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play for purchases, refunds, and subscription management.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when Star Diary changes. Material updates will be reflected on this page before or alongside app release updates.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to hello@choiwon.dev.
